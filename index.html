How Kashi Compass Works

No app to install, no ticket counter to find. Every monument marker unlocks its own living guide, right in your browser.

Step One

Find the bronze Kashi Compass marker at the monument gate and scan it with your phone camera.

Hidden Gems Locals Love

Step past the crowds — these quieter corners of Kashi reward the curious.

Hidden Gem

Man Mandir Observatory

An 18th-century stone observatory built by Maharaja Jai Singh II, with astronomical instruments overlooking the river.

⏱ 20 min🚶 8 min from Dashashwamedh
Hidden Gem

Bharat Mata Mandir

A temple with no deity — instead, a massive marble relief map of undivided India carved into the floor.

⏱ 25 min🚶 12 min from BHU gate
Hidden Gem

Tulsi Manas Mandir

Marble walls engraved with the entire Ramcharitmanas, in the garden where Tulsidas is believed to have lived.

⏱ 30 min🚶 15 min from Durga Kund

Taste of Kashi

Local Food Worth the Walk

Four legendary stops, all within easy reach of the temple.

Blue Lassi Shop

A tiny, tile-walled institution near Manikarnika Ghat, whipping lassi in clay kulhads since 1925.

🚶 5 min from Manikarnika₹60–150
🥭 Must-try: Pomegranate Lassi

Kachori Gali

A cluster of stalls serving crisp kachori with spiced sabzi and jalebi, straight off the griddle since sunrise.

🚶 4 min from Temple₹40–100
🍥 Must-try: Kachori-Sabzi with Jalebi

Jyoti Cafe

A rooftop favourite for backpackers, serving thalis and Ganga views in equal measure.

🚶 7 min from Dashashwamedh₹150–350
🍛 Must-try: Banarasi Thali

Mona Lisa Cafe

A riverside terrace with laid-back seating and slow mornings, popular for its coffee and Aarti views.

🚶 6 min from Dashashwamedh₹120–300
☕ Must-try: Masala Chai & Pancakes
Take Kashi Home

Shopping in the Old City

Banaras has clothed and adorned India for a thousand years — here's where to find the real thing.

Featured Shop

Vandana Silks Banaras

Since 1974, this Girjaghar landmark has been one of Varanasi's most trusted names for pure Katan silk and handloom Banarasi sarees, with generations of weaver families behind every piece. Ask staff to show you the difference between real zari and imitation work — most are happy to explain the craft.

📍 D40/7-A, Girja Ghar Chauraha, Godowlia, Varanasi 221001
📞 +91 70520 00318🕓 Open until 11:00 PM
